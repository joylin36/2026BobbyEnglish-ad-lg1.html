第一條：修業班別與班級性質 / Article 1: Course and Program Enrollment Type：
1. 本契約核定就讀班級為 Let's Go 1 基礎常態進度班。
2. 本課程屬一般系統化正規美語教學進度班，非屬保證班（無特定考取承諾、無退費退分擔保 or 保證期間承諾）。
1. The approved program under this contract is the standard "Let's Go 1 Foundational Progression Class."
2. This course is a regular systematic progression program and is NOT a "guaranteed success" or "warranty" program (No promises of passing specific exams or special refund-guarantee warranties shall apply).
第二條：修業期間與插班計費規範 / Article 2: Study Period and Pro-Rata Later Enrollment Policy：
1. 本期修業期間自民國115年08月31日正式開課起，至課程全期結束止。
2. 課程開始後報名插班者，應以實際報名後之最近一次上課日作為開課日，並以該課程當期剩餘期間及未上課時數，依比例進行學費核算收費。
1. The term of study commences on the official start date of August 31, 2026, until the scheduled conclusion of the entire semester program.
2. For students enrolling after the course has commenced, the immediate next class date after formal registration shall be deemed the official start date, and fees shall be calculated pro-rata based on the remaining lessons and period.
第三條：課程科目、內容與授課時數 / Article 3: Course Subjects, Content, and Timetable Hours：
1. 課程科目與核心內容：Let's Go 1 美語基礎教學（涵蓋 26 字母標準拼讀認讀、Phonics 自然發音進階啟盟、日常情境指令與生活社交對話、高頻率字彙視覺記憶養成）。
2. 授課時數：每週授課 3 次，每次 2 小時，每週共計 6 小時，全期總堂數共 51 堂，確實依據核定課表執行。
1. Course Subjects and Core Content: Let's Go 1 Foundational English (including standard alphabet writing/recognition, advanced Phonics enlightenment, classroom directions, situational daily dialogues, and visual vocabulary high-frequency training).
2. Hours: 3 times a week, 2 hours per lesson, totaling 6 hours per week for 51 lessons in total, executed strictly according to the approved schedule.
第四條：班級編制與實際上課人數上限 / Article 4: Class Size and Legal Capacity Limitations：
1. 本班採取精緻小班制教學，每班核定招生人數上限嚴格遵守政府法令規範上限10人，以確保教學品質與場所公共安全。
1. Exquisite small-class system. The maximum number of students enrolled per class shall strictly comply with government regulatory limitations and is capped at 10 students to ensure teaching quality and venue safety.
第五條：收費項目、繳費總金額與禁止不當收費 / Article 5: Fee Items, Grand Total, and Receipt Requirements：
1. 本期核定費用項目已完整列明於本契約第一項：包含學費、教材講義費及當期學雜費，繳費總金額為新台幣 35,948 元整。
2. 未明列之項目，乙方不得以任何名義另行收取。所有收取費用乙方應依法開立正式核定電子或實體收據憑證。
1. The approved fees are explicitly detailed in Section 1: consisting of Base Tuition, Textbooks/Materials, and Fees, totaling a Grand Total of NT$35,948.
2. Party B shall not collect any fee items that are not explicitly specified herein under any pretext. Official receipts (electronic or physical) must be issued for all fees collected.
第六條：桃園市法定退費標準 / Article 6: Refund Policy and Regulations (Taoyuan City Standard)：
依據「桃園市短期補習班退費管理自治條例」規範，學員因故離班退費標準如下：
(1) 實際開課日前第30日以前要求退費者：退還當期依約繳納費用之總額（100%全退）。
(2) 實際開課日前第1日至第29日要求退費者：退還繳納費用總額之百分之九十（90%）。但所扣除金額以新臺幣一千元為限，超過部分仍應退還。
(3) 實際開課日起5日內要求退費者：退還依約繳納費用總額之百分之八十（80%）。
(4) 實際開課日起第六日，至全期或總課程時數之三分之一期間內要求退費者：退還依約繳納費用總額之百分之心理（50%）。
(5) 已逾全期或總課程時數之三分之一者：所收取之當期費用得全數不予退還。
(6) 代辦之教材費若已購置並已發放予甲方者，該項目不予退還，教材歸甲方所有。
In accordance with the Taoyuan City Cram School Refund Regulations, refunds shall be processed within 7 days based on the following:
(1) Requested 30 days or more before start date: Full 100% refund of total fees paid.
(2) Requested within 1 to 29 days before start date: 90% refund of total fees paid (the amount retained by school is capped at NT$1,000, excess must be refunded).
(3) Requested within 5 days from the start date: 80% refund of total fees paid.
(4) Requested from the 6th day until 1/3 of total course term: 50% refund of total fees paid.
(5) Requested after 1/3 of total course term: Fees collected may be entirely non-refundable.
(6) Issued materials or textbooks are non-refundable and belong to the student.
第七條：學員請假規範與導師編制補課機制 / Article 7: Absence, Leave Notification, and Make-up Tutoring：
1. 學生若因病、因事假無法出席，監護人應於上課前兩小時透過官方 LINE 帳號或專線完成請假登記。
2. 凡完成合規請假者，本班將指派該班導師安排合適時間，提供一對一或小組專業輔導、補課或提供课堂精華。
3. 未依規定請假無故缺課（曠課）者，視同自動放棄該堂課程權益，本班不另行補課，亦不扣除學費或退費。
1. If a student cannot attend due to sickness or personal matters, parents must request leave via official LINE or phone at least 2 hours prior to class.
2. For approved leaves, the school will arrange 1-on-1 or small-group instructor-led make-up classes or supplementary tutoring.
3. Unexcused absences without proper notification constitute a waiver of lesson rights; no make-up sessions or tuition deductions shall apply.
第八條：可歸責乙方之調課補課與退費保障 / Article 8: Alterations Attributable to Party B and Penalty Refund：
1. 因可歸責於乙方之事由（如教師因故請假等）調動上課時間，致甲方無法配合而缺課者，乙方應無條件予以補課，或按比例計算返還該節課程費用。
2. 乙方無正當事由變更原訂課程、科目、時數、上課地點或更換教學人員超過三分之一人數者，甲方得宣告終止契約，乙方應依甲方已繳金額費用加計30%作為懲罰性違約金退還甲方。
1. If a class schedule is adjusted due to reasons attributable to Party B (such as instructor leave), resulting in Party A's inability to attend, Party B shall provide alternative make-up sessions or refund the specific lesson fee proportionally.
2. If Party B alters the original course, subject, hours, or replaces instructors by more than 1/3 without cause, Party A may terminate the contract, and Party B must refund all fees paid plus an extra 30% penalty.
第九條：肖像權合理使用授權與個資隱私安全保護 / Article 9: Portrait Rights and Personal Data Privacy Protection：
1. 甲方同意並授權乙方得於常態教學、成果發表或特殊節慶活動中進行拍照與錄影，並得將學員影像合理、不損及名譽地使用於補習班內部學習成果展示、巴比美語官方網站或招生推估文宣。
2. 乙方應嚴格遵守《個人資料保護法》規範，甲方所填報之所有個資（包含身分證字號、聯絡手機、居住地址等）僅得用於補習班內部教學行政、安全維護與緊急聯絡，未經甲方書面允許，絕不得洩漏、提供給任何第三人或作不當利用。
1. Party A authorizes Party B to take photos and videos during regular classes or holiday events, and reasonably use the images for internal achievement displays or official website promotional marketing, provided it doesn't harm the student's reputation.
2. Party B must strictly handle all personal data (including ID numbers, phone numbers, and addresses) in full compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act solely for academic administration, safety, and urgent communication. Selling or disclosing data to third parties is strictly prohibited.
第十條：違規行為處置、秩序維護與契約終止 / Article 10: Student Conduct, Classroom Order, and Termination：
學員於修業期間應共同維護課堂秩序與安全。若有嚴重干擾他人學習、惡意破壞公共設施、強奪、竊盜或其他違法行為且屢勸不聽者，為維護全班學員安全防線，乙方得單方面終止本契約，並依第六條退費條款辦理剩餘款項退費。
Students must strictly maintain classroom order and safety. For severe disruptions endangering others, malicious vandalism, thefts, or illegal acts where repeated counseling fails, Party B reserves the right to unilaterally terminate this contract to secure class safety, issuing refunds according to Article 6.
