第一條：就讀課程科目內容 / Article 1: Course Content：
本課程為一般系統化正規美語進度班（Let's Go 1 基礎發音拼讀啟盟、日常情境指令理解對話、視覺核心字彙與視覺記憶養成），非屬保證班。
This course is a standard systematic progression class (Let's Go 1 Children's English including Phonics, situational dialogues, listening, speaking, reading, and visual vocabulary) and is NOT a "guaranteed success" program.
第二條：桃園市法定退費標準 / Article 2: Refund Policy (Taoyuan City)：
依據「桃園市短期補習班退費管理自治條例」規定辦理：(1) 開課日前30日以前要退費者退還100%；(2) 開課日前1至29日退費者退還90%（留存上限一千元）；(3) 開課日起5日內要求退費者退還80%；(4) 開課日起第六日至全期三分之一內退費者退還50%；(5) 已逾全期三分之一者，得全數不予退還。(6) 已發放之代辦教材費不予退還。
Refunds comply with Taoyuan City standards: (1) 30+ days before start: 100% refund; (2) 1-29 days before start: 90% refund (school retention capped at NT$1,000); (3) Within 5 days of start: 80% refund; (4) From 6th day until 1/3 of course term: 50% refund; (5) After 1/3 of course term: Non-refundable. (6) Distributed textbooks are non-refundable.
第三條：請假規範與補課機制 / Article 3: Leave and Make-up Lessons：
1. 學生因病、事假缺席，家長須於上課前兩小時透過官方 LINE 或電話完成請假程序。
2. 完成請假者，本班提供由導師安排合適時間進行一對一/小組補課與課後輔導。
3. 未請假曠課者視同放棄堂數，不予安排補課、不扣除學費或退費。
1. Parents must request leave via LINE or phone at least 2 hours prior to class for sickness or personal matters.
2. Approved absences will be offered 1-on-1 or small-group make-up tutorings arranged by the instructor.
3. Unexcused truancy constitutes a waiver of lesson rights; no make-up classes or refunds will be issued.
