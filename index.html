全美語聽不懂？不敢說？巴比老師用滿滿的遊戲，帶孩子在玩樂中無痛解鎖聽、說、讀！

Too shy to speak in an all-English classroom? Teacher Bobby uses fun games to help kids effortlessly unlock listening, speaking, and reading!

No rush to write—our priority is to make children fall in love with their very first steps in English! Through immersive matching, imitating, and repeating activities, we lay a joyful and solid foundation for early English enlightenment.

📝 實用聽說練習精華 | Practical Conversations

  • 學會自信說出自己的名字，並流利詢問與回答生活物品的名稱。
    Learn to say one's own name and fluently ask and answer the names of objects.
  • 熟練基礎社交打招呼，並能主動詢問與描述各種顏色及形狀。
    Master basic greetings and actively ask and answer questions about colors and shapes.
  • 學會大方介紹新朋友，並能準確詢問與回答物品的數量及名稱。
    Learn to introduce friends and accurately ask and answer the quantities and names of items.
  • 溫馨介紹自己和家人，並能運用簡單形容詞描述人們的外表。
    Introduce oneself and family members, and describe people's appearances using simple adjectives.
  • 快樂會說生日快樂、大方詢問他人年紀，並靈活運用基礎形容詞。
    Say happy birthday, ask about age, and flexibly use everyday adjectives.
  • 輕鬆形容天氣狀態、詢問數量，並能準確說出物品擺放的位置。
    Describe the weather, ask about quantities, and state where objects are located.
  • 清楚表達自己想吃的食物，並能熱情詢問體人的飲食喜好。
    Express what food one wants to eat and ask others about their food preferences.
  • 樂於與他人分享並主動詢問彼此最喜歡的顏色與動物。
    Share and ask others about their favorite colors and animals with joy.

📝 核心語法觀念 | Grammar Essentials

精準掌握代名詞、所有格、人稱代名詞與 Be 動詞用法；熟練 Wh-問句、場所介系詞、不定冠詞 (a/an)、指示詞 (this/that/these/those) 以及 there 引導的存在句型。

Master pronouns, possessives, and Be verbs; proficient in Wh-questions, prepositions of place, articles (a/an), demonstratives, and sentences beginning with "there".
